About

Chatims is an anonymous one-on-one chat app. You chat with a random stranger for 7 minutes. If both of you like each other — your real contacts unlock.

How It Works

Set your preferences, start a chat, and enjoy the conversation. If there's a mutual like — you both see each other's contact info.

Privacy & Safety

You stay anonymous until a mutual match. No personal info is shared without consent.
